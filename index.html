Questions

Frequently asked questions

What services does Shreeji Infotech provide?

We offer web development, mobile apps, CRM solutions, digital marketing, AI & automation, cloud services, outsourcing, IT consultancy, and ongoing support & maintenance — all under one roof.

How long does a typical project take?

Small websites or apps typically take 2–4 weeks, while larger platforms like CRMs or e-commerce systems take 6–12 weeks depending on scope and complexity.

Do you offer outsourcing or dedicated teams?

Yes. We provide dedicated developers and full teams on a monthly or project basis, integrated directly with your workflows and tools.

Can you maintain a system built by someone else?

Absolutely. Our support & maintenance team can take over existing websites, apps, and CRMs, handling updates, fixes, and performance improvements.
